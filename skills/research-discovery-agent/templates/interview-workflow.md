# Interview Workflow (Developer & Designer)

## Goals
Validate findings with practitioners and capture missing constraints.

## Steps
1. Warm-up & context
2. Task/environment walkthrough
3. Pain-point deep dive
4. Toolchain details (Xcode, CI, devices)
5. Constraints (perf, privacy, offline)
6. Wishlist and tradeoffs
7. Summarize and confirm next steps

## Artifacts
- Transcripts/notes
- Key quotes
- Decisions and owners
