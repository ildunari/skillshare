> We wanted to reach out to let you know that we will be implementing updates to your account, which may potentially impact your experience, and we sincerely apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.

We’re making an update to your account, and you might notice a few changes while it rolls out. I’m sorry for the inconvenience.

## What changed (high level)

- Made the next step concrete (“reply here”).
