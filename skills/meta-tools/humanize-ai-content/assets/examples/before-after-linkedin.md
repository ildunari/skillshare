# Example: LinkedIn post

## BEFORE (AI-ish)

I’m excited to share that our team is thrilled to announce a new initiative that will revolutionize the way we collaborate. This cutting-edge program will leverage best-in-class processes to drive impactful results across the organization. Stay tuned for more updates as we continue on this journey.

## AFTER (humanized)

We’re kicking off a new collaboration initiative this month.

The goal is straightforward: fewer handoff headaches and faster decisions when projects span teams. We’ll start small, measure what changes, and share what works (and what doesn’t) as we go.

If you’ve been burned by “process improvements” before, I get it. We’re trying to keep this practical.
