**MUST READ** before every use of this skill.

This file captures observed issues, successful patterns, and calibration learnings from distillation runs. Entries are tagged by category and capped at 75. When the cap is reached, merge duplicates, promote recurring patterns to reference files, and archive resolved items.

## Categories

- `[slop]` — slop filter too aggressive or too lax
- `[extraction]` — items missed or incorrectly extracted
- `[quality]` — quality scoring miscalibration
- `[dedup]` — merge/conflict resolution issues
- `[architecture]` — structural template mismatch
- `[polish]` — language, consistency, or loss issues
- `[workflow]` — pipeline flow or working document problems
- `[output]` — final deliverable formatting issues
