| TCA | Medium–High | Very High | Medium | Complex domains, cross-feature state |
| Clean | Medium–High | High | Medium | Enterprise, longevity |
| VIPER | High | High | High | Explicit boundaries per screen |
| Coordinators | Medium | Medium | Low | Flow composition |
